MUSIC USAGE GUIDELINES

  • If you plan to upload a video featuring my music to YouTube, you are free to do so as long as you credit me. However, it may trigger YouTube Content ID. If you would like the copyright claim to be removed, please contact me via email after uploading the video to request removal.

  • If you wish to use my collaborative works with other artists, please contact the other artist(s) for their approval. While I'm already okay with it, their explicit approval is still required.

  • If you want to upload my music to YouTube as a visualizer, please obtain my explicit permission beforehand. Otherwise, any existing copyright claim may remain on your video.

  • For inquiries about using my music in games, or any other questions in general, please contact me at shormty4music@gmail.com or Discord.
